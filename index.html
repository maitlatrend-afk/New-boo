🛡️ Risk Info — Padhna Zaroori Hai
▾
OTC pricing: Pocket Option/Quotex jaisay brokers ke OTC pairs weekend ya off-market hours mein chalte hain. Ye prices broker ke apne algorithm se generate hoti hain — kisi real exchange/interbank market se connected nahi. Matlab koi bhi outside tool (ye app samet) un prices ko predict nahi kar sakta, kyunki wo data kahin publicly available hi nahi hai.
Payout math: Agar win pe ~92% payout milta hai aur loss pe 100% capital jata hai, to breakeven ke liye win rate kam az kam ~52-53% chahiye hoti hai — sirf 50% sahi hona bhi long-term mein nuksaan deta hai.
$10 trade × 100 trades
52 wins × $9.20 = $478.40
48 losses × $10 = $480.00
Net: -$1.60 (even at 52% win rate)
Martingale danger: Loss ke baad stake double karna ek losing streak ko exponentially mehnga bana deta hai. Streaks (5-7 losses lagataar) random chance se bhi hoti hain.
Ye app practice/learning ke liye hai. Real paisay risk karne se pehle: sirf wo amount lagao jiska nuksaan afford kar sako, strict daily loss-limit rakho, aur "signal" ko guarantee mat samjho.
